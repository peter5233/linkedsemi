低功耗蓝牙 LE51 系列芯片



概述

LE51系列是高性能的低功耗蓝牙系统级芯片（SoC），支持最新蓝牙5.1标准，具备蓝牙5.1的AoA（到达角）和AoD（发出角）“寻向”功能，可以应用于物品追踪、高精度室内定位等场景。


Angle of Arrival (AoA / 到达角)

  • Beacon与Locator建立蓝牙连接；
  • Beacon使用单个天线发射信号；
  • Locator使用天线阵列接收信号；
  • 接收信号时因到达时间不同，不同天线上产生相位差，以求Beacon方位；
  • 典型应用是停车场寻车，通过钥匙（Locator）分辨汽车（Beacon）方位；



Angle of Departure (AoD / 发出角)

  • Beacon与Mobile无需建立蓝牙连接；
  • Beacon使用天线阵列发射信号；
  • Mobile使用单个天线接收信号；
  • Mobile获取与各个Beacon的相对角度，综合角度信息计算自己的位置；
  • 典型应用是商场导航，用户手机Mobile扫描Beacon，在地图上求得位置；


技术规格


BLE RF Transceiver

  • 2.4GHz RF transceiver for BLE 5.1;
  • 125K/250K/500K/1M/2M bps data rate;
  • -105dBm RX sensitivity @BLE 125Kbps;
  • Programmable TX power up to +12dBm;
  • Single-ended antenna output;

BLE Protocol

  • BLE 5.1 with AoA / AoD feature;
  • SIG BLE MESH / Proprietary BLE MESH;

Low Power

  • Active-Mode RX: 4.5mA @3.3V;
  • Active-Mode TX: 4.3mA @3.3V 0dBm;
  • Standby: 1µA (RTC running and RAM retention);
  • Shutdown: 30nA (Wake up on GPIO events);

Microcontroller

  • ARM Cortex-M0 32bit MCU;
  • Up to 64MHz clock speed;
  • 256 / 512KB Flash;
  • 32 / 64KB SRAM;

Peripherals

  • Configurable GPIOs;
  • 4 timers (with 1 advanced timer);
  • Complementary pwm output;
  • IWDT / WWDT;
  • Real timer counter (RTC);
  • I2C / SPI / UART;
  • 8 channel 12bit ADC;
  • 12 channel DMA;
  • Integrated temperature sensor;
  • On-chip DC-DC, LDO, POR, PDR;
  • AES/DES, ECC, TRNG;
